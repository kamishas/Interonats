## Responsive Behavior

### Desktop (1200px+)
- 3 columns for role cards
- Side-by-side stats in header
- Full permission descriptions visible

### Tablet (768px - 1199px)
- 2 columns for role cards
- Stacked stats in header
- Abbreviated permission labels

### Mobile (< 768px)
- 1 column for role cards
- Fully stacked layout
- Collapsed permission categories

## Interaction States

### Role Card States
- **Normal**: White background, subtle border
- **Hover**: Light gray background, elevated shadow
- **Selected**: Purple/pink gradient border
- **Disabled**: Gray overlay, reduced opacity

### Button States
- **Primary**: Purple gradient background
- **Secondary**: White with border
- **Danger**: Red text/background
- **Disabled**: Gray background, low opacity

### Permission Toggles
- **ON**: Purple/pink gradient slider
- **OFF**: Gray slider
- **Hover**: Slight scale increase
- **Focus**: Blue outline for accessibility
