# How to Add a Dependent Filing - Step by Step Guide

## Quick Start: 3 Steps to Create Your First Dependent Filing

### Step 1: Add a Dependent to an Employee

1. **Go to Immigration Management** â†’ "All Employees" tab
2. **Click "View Details"** on any employee
3. **Scroll down** to the new "Dependents" section
4. **Click "Add Dependent"** button
5. **Enter dependent's name** (e.g., "Jane Smith")
6. **Enter relationship** (Spouse, Child, Parent, or Other)
7. **Click OK** - The dependent is now added!

You should now see the dependent listed in the Dependents card.

---

### Step 2: Create a Filing for the Dependent

**Option A: From the Employee Detail View**
1. While still viewing the employee, scroll to "Filing History"
2. Click "Add Filing"
3. The employee will be pre-selected
4. **Look for the new "Filing For" dropdown** (appears because dependent exists!)
5. Select the dependent from the dropdown
6. Fill in filing details (type, status, dates, etc.)
7. Save

**Option B: From Visa Filings & Actions Tab**
1. Go to "Visa Filings & Actions" tab
2. Click "Add Filing"
3. Select the employee
4. **The "Filing For" dropdown appears**
5. Select the dependent
6. Fill in details and save

---

### Step 3: View the Dependent Filing

**In the Visa Filings & Actions Table:**
- Look for the "Filing For" column

- Employee filings show: "Primary Employee" (in gray)

**Filter to See Only Dependent Filings:**
- Use the "Person" filter
- Select "Dependents Only"
- See all dependent filings across all employees

## Troubleshooting

### "I don't see the 'Filing For' dropdown"

**Cause:** The employee doesn't have any dependents yet.

**Solution:** Add a dependent first (Step 1 above).

---

### "I can't find the Dependents section"

**Location:** Open any employee's detail view, scroll down past:
- Current Status
- Filing History  
- Green Card Process (if exists)
- **Dependents** â† Here!
- Documents

---

### "I added a dependent but can't see them in the dropdown"

**Try:**
1. Close the Add Filing dialog
2. Close the employee detail dialog
3. Re-open the employee detail
4. Try adding a filing again

The dependent list refreshes when you re-fetch the employee data.
